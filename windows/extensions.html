Extensions

Manage built-in Light Browser protections and access Chrome-compatible extensions. Built-in extensions are part of the browser and cannot be uninstalled.

Built-in Protections
Light AdBlocker Built-in Active
Blocks ads, trackers, and malicious scripts at the network level before they load. Covers 50+ ad networks and YouTube pre-roll ads.
Fingerprint Shield Built-in Active
Spoofs browser fingerprint by normalizing User-Agent headers and removing Client Hints that leak hardware and OS info to websites.
Privacy Headers Built-in Active
Automatically sends Do Not Track signals and removes Electron-specific headers that could identify Light Browser to websites.
HTTPS Upgrader Built-in
Automatically upgrades HTTP connections to HTTPS when available, keeping your traffic encrypted.
